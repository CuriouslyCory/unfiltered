# Analysis of Executive Order—Strengthening and Promoting Innovation in the Nation's Cybersecurity

This Executive Order, signed by Joseph R. Biden, Jr. on January 16, 2025, outlines numerous actions to enhance cybersecurity across the Federal Government and private sector.

## Key Points:

- **Focus on Supply Chain Security:** Requires software providers to attest to secure development practices and submit artifacts for validation.
- **Enhancements to Federal Systems:** Aims to improve identity management, threat detection, cloud security, and space system cybersecurity.
- **Securing Federal Communications:** Mandates stronger authentication, encryption, and routing security for internet traffic, DNS, email, and voice/video conferencing.
