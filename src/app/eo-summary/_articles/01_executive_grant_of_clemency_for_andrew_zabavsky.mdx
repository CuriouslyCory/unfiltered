# Analysis of Executive Grant of Clemency for Andrew Zabavsky

This Executive Order, signed by Donald J. Trump on January 22, 2025, grants a full and unconditional pardon to Andrew Zabavsky for convictions in the United States District Court for the District of Columbia.

## Key Points

- **Grant of Clemency**: The order grants a full and unconditional pardon to Andrew Zabavsky.
- **Convictions**: Zabavsky was convicted of violations of section 371 and 1512 of title 18, United States Code.
- **Sentence**: He was sentenced on September 12, 2024, to 48 months of imprisonment on each count (to run concurrently), 3 years of supervised release, and a special assessment of $200.
- **Pardon Attorney**: The order designates the Pardon Attorney as the President's representative to sign the grant of clemency.
